Choice Task Experiment

This is a jsPsych-based choice task where participants need to find 6 special images (1-6) among 9 total images.

  • Correct images: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6
  • Incorrect images: 7, 8, 9
  • Time limit: 60 seconds
  • Audio feedback: Different sounds for first clicks, repeat clicks, and incorrect images
Start the Experiment

Available Files

  • jspsych_choice_task.html - Main experiment file
  • manifest.json - Lookit study manifest
  • img/ - Image files (1.png, 2.png, etc.)
  • mp3/ - Audio files (correct.mp3, notDiff.mp3, gotItAlready.mp3)
  • ogg/ - Alternative audio format files

How to Use

Click the "Start the Experiment" button above to begin the choice task. The experiment will run for 60 seconds, during which you can click on any image. Correct images (1-6) will play different audio feedback depending on whether it's your first or repeat click on that image.
